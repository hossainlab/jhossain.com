Declaration

This survey focuses on the attitude of students from different universities of Bangladesh towards diabetes and their daily activities, food habits and sleeping pattern. Your kind participation in this survey will help our research. This survey is expected to be spontaneous. You are requested to fill the given questions based on your won knowledge. All the information collected for this survey is highly confidential and will be used for research purpose only.
